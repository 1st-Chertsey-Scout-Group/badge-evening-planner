Run as a role-play. One scout is the 999 operator, another is the caller working
from a scenario card. Cover:

- Why getting help early matters and who to send for it.
- Which service to ask for.
- Giving a clear location and a calm description of what happened.
- Staying on the line and following the operator's instructions.
