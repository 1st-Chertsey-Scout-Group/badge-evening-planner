Panel in a horseshoe, leader as host, buzzer in the middle: every scout sits
as tonight's resident expert on their own favourite sport.

- The 60-second rulebook: each expert explains their sport's rules against
  the clock, then takes the ladder question - how far can you climb, from
  local club through county and national to the world stage? Panel scores
  each out of 5 for "could a beginner now play".
- Mystery bag: pull a prop, first buzz names it and its sport. Each expert
  then lists the kit you would need to start their own sport - bonus point
  if the panel can't add anything they missed.
- Champions round: each expert gives three clues about a personality or
  champion in their sport for the panel to guess, then names the one who
  inspires them and why.
- What happened next: each expert sets up a recent major event, final or
  landmark in their sport and stops just before the key moment - the panel
  guesses, the expert reveals what really happened.
- Quick-fire finish: how do you actually keep up - highlights, podcasts,
  results apps, going to games? One answer each, point for anything genuine.

Totals on the board at the whistle; the champion of champions keeps the buzzer.
