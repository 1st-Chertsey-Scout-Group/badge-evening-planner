Cone a pit box round a leader's car and run the patrol as a race crew,
rotating stations until the timed wheel change ends the session.

- Bonnet briefing: suck-squeeze-bang-blow makes the power; the clutch breaks
  the drive, the gearbox trades speed for pull, and the diff lets the inside
  wheel run slower in corners - prove it with two scouts cornering on a pole.
- Fluids: top up the screen wash; read the coolant between min and max, top
  up cold only - anti-freeze stops a frozen block cracking. Never open a hot
  system.
- Rubber: pressures from the door-sill sticker, gauge and pump to spec; tread
  against the legal 1.6mm with gauge and 20p check; and why unevenly worn
  tyres never share an axle - mismatched grip bites in the wet.
- Spares: swap the wiper blade, a bulb up front and one in the rear cluster.
- Timed wheel change, full crew: chock it, crack the nuts, jack under the
  jacking point, wheel off and on, nuts snugged diagonally, drop, final
  tighten - time on the board.
- Pit pass quiz: the MOT - yearly once a car turns three; name five checks
  (brakes, tyres, lights, steering, seatbelts, emissions) to graduate.

Engine off, key out, handbrake on throughout - and nothing goes under a car
held only by a jack; show the axle stands that would hold it.
