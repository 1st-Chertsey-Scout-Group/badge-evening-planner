title: Problem solving gauntlet
description: Three initiative tests back to back - human knot, magic carpet, toxic waste - then a debrief on the role you played.
duration: 20
equipment:
  - Tarpaulin about 2m square
  - Rope laid as a 3m exclusion circle
  - Bucket half-filled with balls and 6-8 cords about 2m long
  - Blindfolds
  - Planks and crates for the tie-break
  - Stopwatch
tags:

Three impossible jobs, one patrol, no leader help: the gauntlet is beaten by
talking, not muscle, and the clock runs from the first whistle.

- Human knot, five minutes: circle up, reach across, take two different
  people's hands, then untangle into a ring without letting go. Solved fast?
  Blindfold two scouts and run it again.
- Magic carpet, five minutes: the whole patrol stands on the tarp and turns
  it completely over without anyone touching the floor. A foot down costs
  thirty seconds.
- Toxic waste, seven minutes: the bucket sits inside the rope circle and
  nothing living crosses the rope. Move it out using only the cords; a
  tipped bucket means reset.
- Thirty-second role call after each test: freeze and name who did what -
  the planner, the voice, the steady hands, the one who spotted the fix.
- Debrief to finish: every scout names three teams they have been part of
  outside tonight - sports side, school project, family, band - and the job
  they did in each. Was tonight the same role or a new one?

Fastest clean sweep takes the gauntlet; a dead heat goes to a plank-and-crate
river crossing.
