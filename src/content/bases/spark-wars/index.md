title: Spark wars

  - Full water bucket

Matches are confiscated at the gate and paper is contraband: tonight fire
comes from a spark and whatever nature left lying around.

- Earn the kit: strikers stay locked away until the patrol names the ways
  heath and grass fires start - dumped disposable BBQs, campfires left
  smouldering, cigarette ends, sun through broken glass, deliberate
  lighting. Each cause buys an item: striker, fire pan, char cloth.
- Tinder scavenge: collect and rank natural tinder driest-first - birch
  bark, dead grass, thistledown. Dead, dry and off the ground is the rule,
  and a parched field is the same fuel - that's why round one matters. The
  leader grades every bundle before it goes near a spark.
- Build small-to-big in the pan: tinder nest at the centre, pencil-thick
  kindling tepee standing ready beside it.
- Spark wars: strike onto char cloth, drop the glow into the nest, fold and
  blow it to flame. First patrol whose flame still lives at sixty seconds
  with a kindling stick alight takes the round.

Fires live in pans inside the marked circle, the bucket is full before the
first spark, and every fire dies by drown, stir, drown until it is hand-cold.
