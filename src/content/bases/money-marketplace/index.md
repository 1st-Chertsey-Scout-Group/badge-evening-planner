The marketplace opens: pairs get a week's wages, a budget sheet with bills
pre-printed, and stalls full of temptation. Healthiest balance wins.

- Shop the week: bills paid first, then choices - sort needs from wants and
  log every spend. The stalls are priced to drain a careless wallet.
- Blind biscuit test mid-shop: plate A or B, vote, then reveal the prices.
  Is the famous name worth triple? Best value is a decision, not a logo.
- Scam storm: phone-shaped cards rain in - YOU HAVE WON AN IPHONE, a "mate"
  DMing for a loan, a 99p game skin that quietly renews every week. Bin the
  scams to keep your balance; fall for one and the banker collects. Call
  the tells together: urgency, too good to be true, asks for your details.
- Banker's quick-fire: where money lives online - banking apps, game coins
  bought with real pounds, digital currencies that can crash overnight.
- The banker's offer: borrowers flip the card - a 20 pound payday loan
  costs 30 to clear - while savers bank theirs and collect interest on the
  spot. One line to keep: debit is your money, credit is borrowed money.
- Cash-up and feelings audit: balances on the board, then one word each for
  how the scam or the empty wallet felt - spot the trap before it spots you.

Check allergies before the biscuit plates go out.
