The factory is open and the customer is fussy. Each patrol draws two
commission cards - one quick job, one big one - and the leader collects, and
tests, at the end of the shift.

- Draw the commissions: a quick build (mug tree, boot scraper) paired with a
  big one (washing-up stand, camp larder, flagpole). The photo on the card
  is the brief.
- Lashing refresher on the first joint together: square lashing where poles
  cross, diagonal where they would spring apart, clove hitch to start and
  finish - tight wraps, tighter fraps.
- Build both gadgets to the photo. Useful beats pretty: mugs must hang,
  bowls must sit level, food shelves stay off the ground.
- Delivery inspection: the mug tree takes six mugs and a shake, the stand
  takes a loaded bowl, the larder takes the daypack - then the leader leans
  on it. Survives and the commission is paid in points; collapses and it's
  back to the workbench for re-lashing.

Best builds skip the scrap pile and go straight into the kit store for camp.
