A short, discussion-led base. Using scenario cards, ask scouts how they would:

- Introduce themselves and explain what is happening.
- Keep a calm, quiet voice and stay at the casualty's level.
- Avoid crowding and keep bystanders back.
- Keep talking to the casualty until help arrives.
