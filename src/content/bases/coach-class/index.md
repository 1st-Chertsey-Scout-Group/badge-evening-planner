Hand the whistle over: tonight the scouts are the coaches and the leader is
just another body in the squad. Pairs draw a coaching card and run their
slot; the squad scores every session out of 10 for clarity and confidence.

- Every pair opens with the 30-second rulebook: the rules of their sport or
  event, plus the kit and clothing you would turn up in to be ready for it.
- Warm-up pair goes first: pulse raiser round the cones, stretches through
  every major muscle group, then moves specific to their sport -
  demonstrated, counted, corrected like a pro.
- Safety briefing slots: the shot, javelin and long jump cards. Deliver the
  range brief - throwing arcs, nobody walks forward of the line, wait for
  the call, spikes done up, no loose kit, the right footwear and clothing.
  The squad earns a point for spotting any rule the coach missed.
- Kit clinic: brush the boots clean, check the studs, pump the ball to
  pressure - then the "is it dead?" round: worn-flat soles, cracked padding,
  frayed laces. Call what is nearing the end of its life and what replaces it.
- Warm-down pair closes: walk the pulse down, long held stretches, and the
  why - tomorrow's stiffness is tonight's skipped warm-down.

Highest-scored pair are head coaches and pick next week's warm-up.
